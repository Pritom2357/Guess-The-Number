Enter Your Name

::How to play::

..Guess the Number..


1. Please enter your name and press "Enter". Then you will see a pop-up, press "ok".


2. In the next page, you will be given two fields: "Input" and "Result". Enter your guessed number in the "input" field and press "Check" button.


3. If your guessed number is correct, you will see a pop-up, Congrats!! You did it!!!


4. Otherwise, you will see instructions in the "Result" field. If you find it hard, you can always give up. To replay, just reload...


5. keep entering and checking numbers until you find it.....
